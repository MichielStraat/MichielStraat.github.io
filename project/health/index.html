Machine Learning for health

Jan 1, 2019
Processing an image of the retina using a filter selective of blood vessels.

Machine Learning methods for among others computer vision and signal analysis have great potential for improving health care. I have been working on projects related to disease diagnosis of the eye and heart arrhythmia identification. I am currently working on projects concerning sleep disorders.

Michiel Straat
Postdoc in Machine Learning

My research interests include Machine Learning, Computational Intelligence and Statistical Physics of Learning.
